1. What is Euro Deniz IBU?

Euro Deniz IBU is an offshore Bank based in the Turkish Republic of Northern Cyprus, licensed and regulated by the Central Bank of Northern Cyprus with representative office in Nicosia, Cyprus.

2. Why is Northern Cyprus?

Famagusta Free Port and Zone is a governmentally designated area founded to promote export-oriented investment and production, to accelerate foreign direct investment and technology access and to develop international trade, legally deemed to be outside of customs territory of the Turkish Republic of Northern Cyprus, a fenced-in area where normal trade barriers and regulatory requirements do not apply with the goal of facilitating trade and commerce, an area which presents various industrial and commercial incentives and business-friendly regulations.

3. Shareholders of Euro Deniz IBU?

Euro Deniz IBU forms offshore international part of a large financial services group. Throughout its history, starting since 1997, today this huge financial organization with shares holding by Emirate NBD BANK offers a wide range of services in the fields of different businesses and personal needs. Euro Deniz IBU is the best option for people who value the preservation of their capital and who are looking for innovative, safe and comfortable working environment.

4. What types of activities do we accept?

Euro Deniz IBU onboards clients involved in various business activities including high-risk ones. We also accept clients incorporated in numerous jurisdictions and of a wide range nationalities.

5. FATCA/CRS:

FATCA (Foreign Account Tax Compliance Act) and CRS (Common Reporting Standard) are known to be applicable to US and EU citizens respectively created for financial information exchange for tax purposes. The information required to be collected and reported to their tax authority is automatically transferred between the domicile jurisdiction and those other countries engaged into commitment. Designed to increase tax revenue, these rules are not applicable to foreign financial institutions (FFI), such as banks and funds. Euro Deniz IBU can become the good choice for the said purpose. Licensed with non-FATCA/CRS jurisdiction the exchange system allows us to guarantee our clients the confidentiality of sensitive information.

7. Restrictions:

We are unable to accept payments to/from black-listed jurisdictions including North Korea, Iraq and Syria.

8. Can Euro Deniz IBU guarantee the safety of your funds?

Definitely. Euro Deniz IBU is securely connected while transmitting encrypted information of its clients to servers. Working with us you would never have to worry about safety of your funds or personal data.

9. Fast Account Opening:

One of our main priorities is to save your time and create the most comfortable environment for your business practice. That is why with Euro Deniz you will have your account opened within 7 days. In addition to our favorable fees and convenient E-banking, we are offering rapid Due Diligence alongside with standard KYC requirements. You may apply for a Standard Account instantly and feel free to use it in less than a week.

10. Designated IBAN:

Our clients have an opportunity to obtain their own IBAN account for payments in multiple currencies with your own name indicated in the beneficiary field. You can make instant payments to international suppliers and affiliate and easily receive SWIFT/SEPA transfers to your account. You may apply for a Dedicated IBAN immediately and receive it with 21 Business Days.

11. What Currencies are available?

Our main currencies are EUR, USD and GBP. Each client may open an account in various currencies without having to pay an additional fee.

12. How can I withdraw my funds?

You can make an instant outgoing transfer request via E-banking or apply for a Debit Card. Please contact your manager for additional information regarding Debit Card limits.

13. How can I cooperate with Euro Deniz IBU as an Agent?

In order to become our introducer and receive referral commission you may contact one of our Account Managers or Online Support to be provided with an Agent Agreement which is to be signed. Please note that we are unable send our Agents the referral commission unless the Agreement is signed.

The Agent Account Opening if free of charge.

14. Can our customers send/receive funds to/from third parties?

Yes.

15. CrytoCurrency:

Euro Deniz IBU provides its clients with an opportunity to make payments in cryptocurrency without any obstacles that you might be facing today with most of the financial institutions. Our valued customers are able to make transactions from their exchange account to an account in our Bank and the opposite via online banking. That is one of our main advantages on the market that may be a great solution for your business.
