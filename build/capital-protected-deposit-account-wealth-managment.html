  • Capital Protected Deposit Account

With the Protected Deposit Account, you can invest in different markets with DenizBank!

With the Capital Protected Deposit Account, while increasing your returns by investing in stock exchange, FX or commodities, you will not be affected by fluctuations in the index or currency rate.

If the index/currency rate on the term is different from the original value, the difference between will be your return while if it is the other way round, you will protect your capital.

DenizBank offers various protected deposit products for risks of index/currency rate decrease or increase in line with market conditions.

Capital Protected Deposit Account "Against the Risk of Fluctuation in FX" :

With Capital Protected Stock Exchange Deposit Account, even if there is fluctuation in the FX index at the end of the term, you will be eligible to receive the loss stemming from the index as deposit interest at the amount of the pre-determined return share ratio.

Capital Protected Deposit Account "Against the Risk of Fluctuation in Stock Exchange" :

With Capital Protected Stock Exchange Deposit Account, even if there is fluctuation in the Borsa İstanbul index at the end of the term, you will be eligible to receive the loss stemming from the index as deposit interest at the amount of the pre-determined return share ratio.

Capital Protected Deposit Account "Against the Risk of Fluctuation in Gold" :

With Capital Protected Gold Deposit Account, even if there is fluctuation in the Gold index at the end of the term, you will be eligible to receive the loss stemming from the index as deposit interest at the amount of the pre-determined return share ratio.

Features

  • Capital Protected Deposit Accounts can be opened at 6 or 12 month terms.
  • Capital Protected Deposit Accounts are opened by DenizBank at certain periods and for various types of index and announced via DenizBank Branches.
  • The currency of Capital Protected Deposit Accounts can be TL, USD or Euro depending on the type of index opened during that period.
  • Withholding tax on the due date is 12% for TL term deposits, 15% for FX deposits, 10% for long term TL deposits and 13% for FX deposits from 6 months to 1 year.
  • 100.000 TL of time deposit accounts are under the guarantee of Savings Deposit Insurance Fund.

How Can I Carry Out a Transaction?

  • At times when Capital Protected Deposit Accounts are announced, you can apply for Capital Protected Deposit via DenizBank Branches and obtain detailed information on the contents and conditions.
