  • Term Deposit Account

Invest through your time deposit account without being affected by changes in interest rates...

Select the maturity date you wish, capitalize on your savings at Deniz securely...

If you wish to receive risk-free return on your investments in TL and FX, you can open a Term Deposit Account at DenizBank by choosing the most suitable rate and term for you.

If you wish, you may choose maturities of 1, 3, 6 and 12 months or choose the duration you want from 1 day to 365 days and you can create a "Broken Term Deposit" account.

In addition, with the Deposit on the Web, which you can open through our Internet branch, you may benefit from higher interest rates compared to our branches.

How can I open an account?

You can easily open a Term Deposit Account at various terms and amounts at our branches, DenizBank internet branch or 08502220800 DenizBank Call Center.
