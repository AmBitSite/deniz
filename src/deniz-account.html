Withdraw and deposit money as you wish

And without even losing your interest!

With Deniz Account you can withdraw and deposit money any time you want without losing your interest.

Features:

  • Lower limit for opening Deniz Account is 1.000 TL/USD/EUR.
  • During 30 days since account is opened you will benefit from favorable welcome interest. Welcome Period Interest ratio can be used only once for each one type of currency.
  • Upper limit for benefiting from welcome interest is 100.000 for TL and 25.000 for USD and EUR.
  • When period of welcome interest is over, during one month you will benefit from fixed interest ratio.
  • You can withdraw and deposit money on Deniz Account any time you want. During these transactions you won’t lose your interest.
  • Even in case of zero balance account your Deniz Account will not close and will keep open until close instruction is given. But in case that your account remains inactive with zero balance for 90 days, it will be closed automatically.
  • You can make EFT, transfer and virement transactions from Deniz Account to other accounts.
  • EFT and transfer can be made to Deniz Account.
  • If your account balance exceeds upper limit, limit excess interest ratio which is lower than standard interest ratio will be applied to your account.

How can I carry out a transaction?

You can open a Deniz Account via AçıkDeniz İnternet Banking or in the nearest branch of DenizBank.
