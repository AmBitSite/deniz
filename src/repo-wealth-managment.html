  • Repo

It is the transaction of selling a Treasury bill or government bond with the commitment to repurchase at the end of a certain period. You can have the opportunity to capitalize on your savings with terms beginning from 1 day.

Features

  • Return on Repo is definite beforehand.
  • Repo interest income is subject to 15% stoppage.
  • The Repo rates announced at our Bank's branches and DenizBank internet branch are net rates.
  • The Repo rates of our Bank can vary depending on the amount that is subject to repo.

How can I carry out a transaction?

  • You can make your investment as overnight (1 day) and weekly (7 day) terms.
  • The minimum limit to carry out a Repo transaction is TL 100.
  • You only need to have a Demand Deposit Account to make a Repo transaction.
  • You can place your Repo demands between 09:30 â€“ 14:00 on week days.
  • You can make transactions via our Branches or DenizBank internet branch.
