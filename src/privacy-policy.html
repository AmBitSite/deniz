At Euro Deniz IBU in order to provide the best products and services, our bank only gathers the information that is necessary and obligatory according the international banking security. According to the security conditions of Euro Deniz IBU, all customers’ requests are confidential. Euro Deniz IBU personnel access to customer information is regulated by Euro Deniz IBU Security Policies and is not shared with any institution and anybody other than the obligatory cases stipulated in the legislation. Euro Deniz IBU employees are obliged to keep confidentiality and secret keeping rules. Respective article can be found within the framework of Euro Deniz IIBU Security Policies. Customer shall provide Euro Deniz IBU internet with accurate personal details to access his/her digital information through the bank’s online platform. The accuracy of data provision is fully customer’s responsibility. At Euro Deniz IBU we provide the security of customers’ information within the framework of Famagusta Court of Turkish Republic of Northern Cyprus. Due to its banking license and jurisdiction of incorporation Euro Deniz IBU is non-CRS compliant, its customers’ information is not transferred to any third parties other than Turkish Republic of Northern Cyprus.
