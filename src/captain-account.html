Capitalize on your savings kept in demand deposit through Captain Account!

You can relax while Captain Account manages your savings. Also, you can carry on with your daily transactions at ease.

  • The Captain Account is an account that continuously invests in your place when money enters your account, evaluates your money as your preference, using Deniz Portfolio short-term Bills&Bonds Fund, Overnight Term Deposit * or foreign currency and makes your life easier while your money keeps increasing its value. Moreover, regular payments such as invoices and credit cards that you have instructed will be made automatically from the Captain Account, so you will be comfortable.
  • Your amount will be determined by your daily cash requirement (with a minimum amount of TL 1.500) and will be made available to your demand account.
  • According to your balance preference over this amount, the Deniz Portfolio Short-term Bills&Bonds Fund is considered as Overnight Term Deposit or foreign currency.
  • When there is insufficient balance in your demand account in cash withdrawals from ATM; without having to make funds and currency exchange transactions; with the automatic fund and currency decay feature, you can withdraw as much money as you can from your fund and / or currency.
