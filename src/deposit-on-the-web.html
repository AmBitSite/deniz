Web Deposit, the deposit that makes you earn with each click

Capitalize on your savings without having to go to the branch, with the advantageous interest rates of internet banking

With Deposit on the Web, you can open term deposit accounts with advantageous interest rates special to the internet branch and receive more return on your deposit.

Features

  • For the term deposit accounts in TL/USD/EUR you will open on the internet branch, the advantageous interest rates determined for different term and amounts will apply special to internet branch.
  • For all foreign currencies the account minimum opening limit is 1.000. For gold it is over 10 grams.
  • The Deposit on the Web account which you open at the internet branch will be automatically renewed with the advantageous interest rate special to the internet branch.

How can I open an account?

After logging into the AçıkDeniz internet branch, you can open your Term Deposit account by clicking "Term Deposit Account" under "Investment Transactions".

  • If you are registered on the internet banking or have any DenizBank cards;
    • With your e-password and açık deniz password,
    • Through the card log in step with your card information, you can easily log in to the AçıkDeniz internet branch.
  • If you are not registered in the internet banking, or you do not have a DenizBank card;
    • You can register in the internet banking at your nearest branch and benefit from advantageous interest rates.
