Thanks to the Time Deposit enabling money withdrawal, you can withdraw money from your principal amount within limits set by our bank without having to wait for or changing your maturity date. Advantages:

  • You can withdraw money in cash for urgent needs without having to wait for maturity date. The remaining principal amount will continue to accumulate interest until maturity date.

  • You can get the full amount of the accumulated interest in case of withdrawals that you will make for long periods of 1 year. *
