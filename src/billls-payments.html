Bills & Payments

You can make all your regular payments without any hassle by virtue of any automatic payment instructions linked to your account with DenizBank. Even if the balance of your account is insufficient, your payments will be carried out given that you have an overdraft account.

Bill Payments

You can pay your electricity, water, telephone, natural gas etc bills via our branches, Açıkdeniz Internet Branch and Açıkdeniz Telephone Banking by automatic payment orders you give to your account.
  • Your bill payments are collected free of charge and automatically from your account on the last payment date of each month through the automatic payment order you give to our bank.
  • Even if there is no money in your account, in the event that you define Rescue to your account all your bill payments will be made without delay.

School Fees/Tuition

DenizBank School Payment System involves many advantages for you and for the school management. These are:
  • Thanks to the overdraft feature of your account, the installments of your children's schoo mfees will be paid on time even if your account has insufficient balance.
  • Free life insurance for your child
  • Salary payments, debit cards, overdraft accounts and credit cards for school employees
  • Credit cards and all banking services available for parents
