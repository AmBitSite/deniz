Double investment opportunity through a single account: DepositFUND!

Benefit from advantageous interest rates for your time deposit while a part of your savings accumulated in a mutual fund

With DepositFUND of Deniz, while a part of your savings accumulate in a mutual fund, you can benefit from advantageous interest rates for your time deposit.

Benefit from higher time deposit interest rates and vary your savings through any of the options of Deposit FUND at DenizBank.

Advantages

  • With DepositFUND, you can capitalize on your savings in the mutual fund you wish in line with your risk and return expectations.
  • You can benefit from more advantageous interest rates compared to a standard time deposit thanks to the DepositFUND you choose.

Features

  • DepositFUND accounts can be opened with weekly, 3-6 monthly and annual maturities.
  • DepositFUND minimum limit is 10,000 TL.

How can I transact?

You can open a DepositFUND through Açıkdeniz internet banking or by visiting the closest Denizbank branch to you.

*Amount up to 100.000 TL in your DepositFUND is in scope of saving deposit insurance.
