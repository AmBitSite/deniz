Protection of Personal Data

Euro Deniz IBU values security of its customers and would like to notify you about “the Law on Protection of Personal Data” introduced for protection the privacy of personal life, human rights and freedom.

Our goal is to provide you the clearest information on how your personal data is acquired, the purposes of processing this data, the related legal reasons and your rights to satisfy customer’s goals.

All types of personal and biometric data terms, health data, corporate, commercial and other data, information and documents (“Data”), as per Law on Protection of Personal Data No. 6698 which entered into force on 7th of April, 2016, Euro Deniz IBU, as Data Controller, may access, record, support, update personal data to continue its services within the capacity of the law, reorganize and disclose, transfer and share data to/with third parties in cases and to the extent permitted by legislation, transfer, convey, share, classify, make anonymous and process the data in other formats prescribed in the law.

Processing your personal data is aimed to include the services that may be provided as agency under the Banking Law and other legislation, as well as: serving, executing and upgrading all kinds of products and services related to the banking, insurance and finance, carrying out promotion, marketing and campaigns for these products, fulfilling terms in agreements that are signed/shall be signed, carrying out intelligence, data research and credibility assessments, planning, statistics and customer satisfaction studies, maintaining security and complying with the liabilities of preserving, reporting, notifying the data set forth by anti-money laundering and national and international legislation, BRSA, CBRT, CMB, FCIB, Under-secretariat of Treasury and other authorized bodies to better serve our customers, develop applicable products and services and provide constant services.

Persons/institutions to whom your personal data may be transferred for purposes mentioned above: your personal data may be shared with subsidiaries of our bank and their sub-institutions; employees, officers, legal, financial and tax consultants, auditors, consultants, institutions, parties the bank collaborates with, to complete or extend services and support service companies and contracted offices including Credit Bureau and FINDEKS, card payment system companies including Europay INT.SA, Moneygram, Mastercard INT.INC, Visa INT, JCB INT, Maestro, Electron that are established within the country or internationally, BRSA, CMB, CBT, MASAK, Banks Association of Turkey, KOSGEB, income administration, under-secretariat of Treasury, SGK, ministries, authorized public bodies like judicial bodies, correspondent banks and national/foreign financial institutions and national/foreign merchants if necessary, persons, institutions and bodies that legislation including article 73/4 of Banking Law permits besides third parties whom you precisely approve.

Your rights as per article 11 of Law; By applying to our Bank, you have the right to a) request whether your personal data was refined, b) inquire information if your personal data was processed, c) get to know the purpose of the processing of your personal data and whether it was used appropriately; ç) find out local and foreign third parties to whom your personal data was forwarded; d) ask for the correction of any incorrect or missing personal data, e) request the erasing or discontinuance of use of your personal data as per terms given in article 7 of Law, f) request that the operations done as per paragraphs (d) and (e) above by means of transferring your personal data to third persons be notified to you, g) object to any situation against your will as your personal data are analyzed by entirely automated systems and h) demand compensation for losses in case of any loss due to processing of your personal data by violation of Law.

Implementation of your rights is possible after the executive date of the regulation which is 07.10.2016 and our rights are reserved to request costs to be incurred by our Bank to fulfill your requests from you as per tariff given in article 13 under Law on Protection of Personal Data entitled “Applying to data keeper.

Criteria that do not require agreement, as per paragraph 2 of article 5 of Law numbered 6698 on Protection of Personal Data, under the condition that it is explicitly foreseen in law and be directly linked to establishment or implementation of an agreement; in cases when personal data related to agreement parties needs to be processed, data keeper Euro Deniz IBU is required to fulfill this legal obligation, when it is made public by the related person, data processing is obligatory to establish, use or protect a right, data processing is obligatory for the legal benefits of the Bank as the data keeper to not harm essential rights and freedom of persons and exemptions provided in articles 73/4 in Banking Law, the Bank keeps the right to process the personal data without obtaining explicit consent.

To fulfill data that is obliged to be disclosed to public as in terms of Law or given in official registries or balance sheets and activity reports and for the Bank to fulfill legal obligations resulting from legislation; and/or data disclosure, usage and transfers are not subject to confidentiality obligation of the Bank in order to fulfill data transfer obligation to persons who may request the secrets due to legal reasons and/or Laws; and the bank is authorized to disclose and give to related persons, process and transfer said data without any need for a separate letter of consent are the key principles of clarity in texts that are published or disclosed or given in Laws.

Our Duty of Confidentiality resulting from Law; as per article 42 of Law numbered 5411 on Banking and article 17 of Regulation on Terms and Conditions of Accounting Principles of Banks and Keeping Documents; is a legal obligation to store your information and documents in our bank for 10 years.

If the client asks the cancellation or termination of personal data, your request shall be executed at the end of 10 years.

Even after this time period finished bank is authorized to analyze your data to help secure banking services improvement and safety further. Your personal data out of the scope mentioned above shall be deleted upon your request.
