Capitalize on your savings at Deniz with Gold Time Deposit

Benefit from interest income while saving in gold

With Gold Time Deposit Account you can use your savings in gold with no risk of theft, loss, fraud. You can benefit from your gold savings interest and at the same time enjoy your secure investment.

If you wish you can open an account of 1 or 3-month maturity or you can open an ‘‘Irregular Maturity Account’’ choosing period from 1 up to 400 days.

Features:

  • Gold Time Deposit Account can be opened with the minimum of 10 gr. of gold, you can invest in 1 gr. or its multiples.
  • Gold Time Deposit Account provides an opportunity to obtain interest yield and benefit from the rise of gold.
  • You will buy and sell gold securely without having to pay labour cost.
  • You can accumulate gold with no risk of theft, loss, and fraud.
  • 100.000 TL portion of each Gold Time Deposit Account is subject to Savings Deposit Insurance.

How can I open an account?

You can visit Denizbank Branch by your nearest DenizBank Branch or you can open a Gold Time Deposit Account through 08502220800 Open Bank Telephone Banking if you have a demand gold account from AçıkDeniz Internet Banking.
